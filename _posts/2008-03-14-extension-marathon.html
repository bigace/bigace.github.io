I had a lot of ideas in mind, for improvements on the existing extensions. But since I am studying and my exams take a lot of time, the progress is slower than I want it to be... but it goes on ;-D

To give you a short idea of the work I did in the days since BIGACE 2.4 was released, you could have a look in the Changelog or you click through the full list of available Extensions.

You can also have a look at the Sourceforge Download Page where all downloads ever released for BIGACE can be found. There is an extra package for the latest extensions (100% compatible). Another improvement, because I need your feedback about Bugs and even more important about required improvements, is the new started download package for Beta versions. You are invited to download and test them and to give me your feedback and ideas!

If you do not know how to install themes, modules (simply every kind of extension), you could have a look at the User Manual about Updates.

All below mentioned Extensions for BIGACE 2.4 where improved, you can have a look at their detail pages and check their Changelog for more information.
