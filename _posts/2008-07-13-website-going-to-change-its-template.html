The most important change is the new header. Even if I got really stuck to the old green design, it was time for a change.

These three points were the most important ones, when working on the new design:

  • The "language chooser/switch" was not obvious to find and many people asked me why there is no german version available
  • Have the most important pages always available at a click
  • A more professional look, while at the same time giving the feeling of an "easy to use" application

This new design is just the starting point for a bigger customization of the current BIGACE website. But unless there is nothing to see, I will keep this secret ;-) So subscribe to the News feed, to make sure you won't miss the next upcoming details.
